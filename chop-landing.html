Why Chop?

🔑 Experts aren't users

Your experts don't need accounts. Each gets a unique token link — open it, answer, done. No friction, no onboarding.

🧠 The skip button is the most important feature

If an expert doesn't know, they skip. No pressure, no filler. Every answer is genuine signal.

📦 Output that works

Synthesis produces context-engineered Markdown — structured for LLMs and humans. Ready to drop into any knowledge base.

🪓 Separate the hats

AI pushes depth and coverage. Humans just answer. The framework keeps the two roles distinct.

🔄 Multiple experts, one capture

Same questions → multiple perspectives. Synthesis finds consensus, flags conflicts, and tags gaps — all from one session.

⚡ Built for async

Capture happens in the gaps — coffee breaks, commutes, idle moments. Five minutes per session. Compound over time.
